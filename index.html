e-Tolbi, the Tool for Smart
and Sustainable Agriculture

Monitor millions of hectares of plantations in
real-time with our AI-based tool and satellite
imagery to estimate and predict yields,
calculate input needs (fertilizers, seeds), and
anticipate risks related to climate hazards,
diseases, and pests.

Sustainable GHG
management

Champion your environmental responsibilities
with our Scope 3 GHG Estimation & Forecasting
tool. By utilizing a comprehensive combination
of local ground truth data, multi-satellite
sensors including LIDAR, RADAR, and optical
imagery, and AI, our system allows you to
monitor greenhouse gas emissions throughout
your supply chain

ADVANCED DATA
MANAGEMENT

Elevate your precision agriculture practices with
our cutting-edge DMRV System. Seamlessly
streamline data collection, analysis, and
reporting using a blend of local ground truth
data, multi-satellite sensors like LIDAR, RADAR,
and optical imagery, combined with the power
of AI. This holistic approach enhances your
operational efficiency and crop management,
empowering you with data-driven insights that
drive improved decision-making.
